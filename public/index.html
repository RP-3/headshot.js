HeadShot.js

Click on any two contrasty parts of your face to activate tracking; nostrils work best--eyes sometimes blink too much. The green dots on the left indicate a lock on your face. When you're ready, scroll down and hit 0.

Got it? Pick a difficulty -- ']' for harder, '[' for easier -- and start shooting (spacebar to shoot). Or hit 1 to instantiate some targets for practice. Remember, you can't stray off camera!
